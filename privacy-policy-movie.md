# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## 1. Introduction

TaekTwo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** (required for account creation and authentication)
- **Username** (chosen by you for display purposes)
- **Display name** (your preferred name for the platform)
- **Profile photo** (optional, uploaded by you)
- **Bio and description** (optional personal information you choose to share)
- **Location** (optional, if you choose to share it)
- **Website** (optional, if you choose to share it)

### 2.2 Content You Create
We store content you create and share on the platform:
- **Posts and reviews** about movies and TV shows
- **Comments and replies** on posts
- **Chat messages** sent through our messaging system
- **Polls and surveys** you create
- **Photos and images** you upload
- **Favorite movies and TV shows** you add to your lists
- **Watchlist items** you save
- **Currently watching** status updates

### 2.3 Usage Data
We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App:
- **App usage patterns** (features used, time spent, navigation patterns)
- **Device information** (device type, operating system, app version)
- **IP address** (for security and analytics purposes)
- **Crash reports and error logs** (to improve app stability)
- **Performance metrics** (loading times, response times)

### 2.4 Communication Data
- **Push notification tokens** (FCM tokens for sending notifications)
- **Email communications** (account-related emails, notifications)
- **Support requests** and communications with our team

### 2.5 Social Interaction Data
- **Followers and following lists**
- **Likes and reactions** on posts and comments
- **Shares and interactions** with other users' content
- **Community memberships** and group participation

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Core App Functionality
We use your information to:
- **Provide the service** (display your profile, posts, and interactions)
- **Enable social features** (following, messaging, community participation)
- **Personalize your experience** (recommendations, content feeds)
- **Maintain your account** (authentication, profile management)

### 3.2 Communication
We use your information to:
- **Send notifications** about posts, messages, and app updates
- **Provide customer support** and respond to your inquiries
- **Send important updates** about the service or these policies

### 3.3 Analytics and Improvement
We use your information to:
- **Analyze usage patterns** to improve the App
- **Identify and fix bugs** and technical issues
- **Develop new features** based on user needs
- **Measure app performance** and user engagement

### 3.4 Safety and Security
We use your information to:
- **Prevent abuse** and enforce our Terms of Service
- **Detect and prevent fraud** or malicious activity
- **Protect user safety** and maintain platform integrity
- **Comply with legal obligations**

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 Public Information
The following information is visible to other users:
- **Username and display name**
- **Profile photo and bio**
- **Posts and reviews** you create
- **Comments and replies** you make
- **Public profile information** (followers count, join date, etc.)

### 4.2 Private Information
We do not share your private information except in these limited circumstances:
- **With your consent** (when you explicitly agree)
- **For legal compliance** (when required by law or court order)
- **For safety reasons** (to protect users from harm)
- **With service providers** (who help us operate the App)

### 4.3 Third-Party Services
We share limited information with:
- **Firebase/Google** (for authentication, database, and storage services)
- **Trakt.tv** (for movie and TV show data - no personal information shared)
- **Analytics providers** (for app improvement - anonymized data only)

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage
Your data is stored using:
- **Firebase Firestore** (for user profiles, posts, and chat messages)
- **Firebase Storage** (for profile photos and post images)
- **Firebase Authentication** (for account security)
- **Local device storage** (for app preferences and cached data)

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement security measures including:
- **Encryption in transit** (HTTPS for all data transmission)
- **Encryption at rest** (Firebase provides encryption for stored data)
- **Access controls** (limited access to user data)
- **Regular security audits** and monitoring

### 5.3 Data Retention
We retain your data:
- **While your account is active** (until you delete your account)
- **For legal compliance** (as required by applicable laws)
- **For safety purposes** (to prevent abuse and protect users)

### 6.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- **View your data** (access your profile and content)
- **Update your information** (edit profile, change settings)
- **Delete your account** (remove your profile and most content)
- **Export your data** (request a copy of your data)

### 6.2 Privacy Settings
You can control:
- **Profile visibility** (public or private profile)
- **Notification preferences** (types of notifications you receive)
- **Content sharing** (who can see your posts and activity)
- **Data collection** (some analytics can be disabled)

### 6.3 Communication Preferences
You can:
- **Unsubscribe from emails** (except essential account communications)
- **Disable push notifications** (through device settings)
- **Control notification frequency** (in app settings)
